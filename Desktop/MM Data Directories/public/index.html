About the Project

About M.E.S.S.I

Myanmarsar Ecosystem for Science, Social and Intelligence

Project M.E.S.S.I is an open ecosystem built to centralise Myanmar's data, research, and AI resources — making them accessible for researchers, developers, students, and journalists across the country and beyond.

Myanmar's AI and data landscape is growing rapidly, yet resources remain scattered across dozens of repositories, research papers, and private drives. M.E.S.S.I — Myanmarsar Ecosystem for Science, Social and Intelligence — was created to solve this: a single, curated directory of open datasets, language models, NLP tools, academic papers, and learning guides, all focused on Myanmar.

The project is maintained independently and built by the community, for the community. If you have a dataset to contribute or a correction to suggest, use the Contribute page.
